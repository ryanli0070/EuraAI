## Description (max 4000 characters)

> **Eura is a math tutor that works the way you do — by hand.**
>
> Instead of typing equations, you write and draw your math on a freehand canvas, just like on paper. Orion — your AI tutor inside Eura — reads your handwriting, checks your work step by step, and gives you guided hints when you're stuck — so you learn the *how*, not just the answer.
>
> **Built for iPad and Apple Pencil**
> A fast, natural drawing canvas with pressure-sensitive strokes. Sketch your work, scribble, and think on the page.
>
> **Understands your handwriting**
> Advanced AI reads what you've written, recognizes the math, and follows your reasoning across multiple steps.
>
> **Checks your steps, not just answers**
> Orion checks each line of your work and points out exactly where things go wrong — with Socratic hints that help you figure it out yourself.
>
> **Ask follow-up questions**
> Stuck on a concept? Chat with the tutor right next to your work for explanations and worked examples.
>
> **Stay organized**
> Group your work into canvases and folders, synced to your account so it's there on every device.
>
> **Private by design**
> No ads. No third-party tracking. Your work is yours, and you can delete your account and data anytime from within the app.
>
> Eura is a study aid that helps you build real understanding — for homework, test prep, and everyday practice.
>
> _Requires an account (email sign-up). For ages 13 and up._
